'Raat' talks about 'Shab-E-Firaaq' // 'The Night of Separation'. Of a bleak night, when time is not your alibi. When a lifetime feels like a second, and a second chooses to live in you for a lifetime. Of impending separation, that is palpable enough for the room to have taken a note of it. The air to have turned cold, to let you know of what entails. Of numerous attempts, of persuasion, of not letting the night end, turning futile. Of the fears, regrets, questions and a life that follows.

BIO

Ankur Chavda is a singer-songwriter from Ahmedabad, India. A software engineer by profession, music has always been a safe space for him. He writes songs to give his vulnerabilities, emotions & life stories a tangible shape and form. With music that's acoustic and guitar-laden, his lyrics are open-ended as he chooses to let his listeners connect with his music with their own story.
